About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at L3S Reseach Center, Leibniz Univeristy Hannover, Germany. I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Informatics (2013) and a Master's degree in Mathematical Foundations for Computer Science (2016) from VNU University of Science, Hanoi, Vietnam. Later, I received my Ph.D. (2024) from the faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology, Leibniz University of Hannover, Germany. My interests lie in LLMs, Generative AI, Natural Language Processing, Multimodal Data Mining, Social Media Mining, AI for Social Good, Explainable AI.
